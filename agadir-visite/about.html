Our story

A Local Agency, A Genuine Welcome

Agadir Visite was born from a simple idea: that visitors deserve to discover the real Morocco — its landscapes, its people and its traditions — in comfort and safety, guided by locals who truly love their country.

Over two decades we have welcomed travellers from across the world, designing original and unique excursions, circuits and transfers. We don't sell a catalogue; we build a journey around you.

Today we remain proudly independent and family-run, recognised by Tripadvisor's "Travellers' Choice" award — but our greatest reward is still a guest who leaves as a friend.

Questions & Answers

Good to Know Before You Book

Do I need to pay in advance?

No. With Agadir Visite you pay on arrival — in cash or by card when you meet your guide or driver. We simply ask you to confirm your booking so we can reserve your place.

Is hotel pickup included?

Yes. Pickup and drop-off at your hotel or the airport is included in every excursion, circuit and activity, across Agadir and Taghazout.

Which languages do your guides speak?

Our guides and drivers speak English, French, German and Spanish. Let us know your preference when booking.

Can you create a custom itinerary?

Absolutely. Tell us your dates, interests and group size and we'll design a tailor-made programme with no obligation.

Are the tours suitable for families and children?

Yes — most of our excursions are family-friendly, and free child seats are available on request for transfers.

What is your cancellation policy?

Because there is no advance payment, you can adjust or cancel free of charge up to 24 hours before departure. Just send us a message.
